About Sidhu Moosewala

Sidhu Moosewala is a popular Punjabi singer, songwriter, and actor from Punjab, India. He has gained immense popularity among Punjabi music lovers for his unique style of singing, which blends traditional Punjabi folk music with contemporary sounds. Sidhu Moosewala has released several hit songs that have become anthems for Punjabi youth worldwide. Some of his popular songs include "So High," "Warning Shots," "Ishq Da Uda Adaa," "Same Beef," and "Dhakka." He has also collaborated with many other well-known Punjabi singers and music producers, such as Amrit Maan, Karan Aujla, and Bohemia, among others. In addition to his singing career, Sidhu Moosewala has also appeared in several Punjabi movies, including "Yes I Am Student" and "Teri Meri Jodi." Despite some controversies surrounding his lyrics, Sidhu Moosewala remains one of the most popular and influential Punjabi singers of his generation. He has won several awards for his music, including the BritAsia Music Award for Best Male Act in 2020.
